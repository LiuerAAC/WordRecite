# 10.7 Nouns That Change Their Vowels In The Plural

## Source Correspondence

This section introduces nouns whose plural form includes a stem-vowel change in addition to, or instead of, a plural ending.

## Pattern

| Singular | Plural | Note |
|---|---|---|
| man | män | vowel change |
| bok | böcker | vowel change + ending |
| hand | händer | vowel change + ending |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

部分名词复数不仅加词尾，还改变词干元音，如 `man -> män`, `bok -> böcker`。这些需要单独记忆。

### 检查点

- 是否能识别 vowel change plural？
- 是否能记住 `man/män`？
- 是否把这类名词列入特殊复数表？
