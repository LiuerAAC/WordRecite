# 10.9 Plurals: Summary

## Source Correspondence

This section summarizes the plural system and reinforces the practical point that plural forms must be learned with nouns.

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

复数总结的关键不是死背规则，而是给每个名词建立完整形态表：不定单数、定单数、不定复数、定复数。
