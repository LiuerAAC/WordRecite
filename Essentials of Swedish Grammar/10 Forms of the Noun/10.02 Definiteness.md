# 10.2 Definiteness

## Source Correspondence

This section explains indefinite and definite noun forms. Swedish indefinite articles come before nouns, while definite articles are usually attached to nouns as suffixes.

## Forms

| Gender | Indefinite | Definite | English |
|---|---|---|---|
| en word | en bil | bilen | a car / the car |
| ett word | ett hus | huset | a house / the house |

## Ending Pattern

| Type | Definite Ending |
|---|---|
| en words | `-en` or `-n` |
| ett words | `-et` or `-t` |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

不定形式用 `en/ett + noun`，定形式通常把定冠词接到名词后：`en bil -> bilen`, `ett hus -> huset`。

### 检查点

- 是否能从 `en bil` 得到 `bilen`？
- 是否能从 `ett hus` 得到 `huset`？
- 是否知道定冠词和 `en/ett` 有关？
