# 10.3 Use Of The Definite And Indefinite Forms

## Source Correspondence

This section discusses when definite and indefinite forms are used. Swedish generally parallels English, but there are differences, especially with general reference, possessive-like contexts, and nouns already identifiable from context.

## Basic Contrast

| Indefinite | Definite |
|---|---|
| introducing something new | referring to something known/identified |
| `en bil` - a car | `bilen` - the car |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

不定形式用于新引入或非特指对象，定形式用于已知、可识别或上下文中特指对象。瑞典语与英语大体相似，但具体用法需要通过例句积累。

### 检查点

- 是否能区分 new information 和 known information？
- 是否能解释为什么要用 definite form？
- 是否注意 Swedish/English 并非总是一一对应？
