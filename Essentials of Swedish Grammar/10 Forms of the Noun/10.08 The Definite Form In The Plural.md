# 10.8 The Definite Form In The Plural

## Source Correspondence

This section explains how plural nouns become definite. The definite plural form is built from the plural form plus a definite ending.

## General Pattern

```text
plural form + definite plural ending
```

| Indefinite Plural | Definite Plural | English |
|---|---|---|
| flickor | flickorna | the girls |
| stolar | stolarna | the chairs |
| banker | bankerna | the banks |
| äpplen | äpplena | the apples |
| hus | husen | the houses |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

定复数是在复数形式基础上再加定性词尾。常见结尾包括 `-na`, `-a`, `-en`，具体随复数类型变化。

### 检查点

- 是否能从 `stolar` 得到 `stolarna`？
- 是否能从 `hus` 得到 `husen`？
- 是否知道 definite plural 不是直接从单数构成？
