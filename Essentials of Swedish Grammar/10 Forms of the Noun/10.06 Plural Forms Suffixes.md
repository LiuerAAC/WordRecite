# 10.6 Plural Forms: Suffixes

## Source Correspondence

This section organizes plural suffixes more explicitly. The plural suffix is attached to the noun stem, sometimes with spelling or vowel adjustments.

## Suffix Table

| Singular | Plural | Suffix |
|---|---|---|
| flicka | flickor | `-or` |
| stol | stolar | `-ar` |
| bank | banker | `-er` |
| äpple | äpplen | `-n` |
| hus | hus | zero |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

复数词尾是名词学习的核心之一。每个名词最好直接记 singular + plural，不要只记规则。
