# 10.5 The Plural Forms Of Nouns

## Source Correspondence

This section introduces Swedish plural classes. Swedish does not have one single plural ending equivalent to English `-s`; plural formation depends on noun type.

## Main Plural Types

| Ending | Typical Use | Example |
|---|---|---|
| `-or` | many en words ending in `-a` | flicka -> flickor |
| `-ar` | many en words | stol -> stolar |
| `-er` | many en words | bank -> banker |
| `-n` | many ett words ending in vowel | äpple -> äpplen |
| zero plural | many ett words ending in consonant | hus -> hus |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

瑞典语复数有多个类型。新名词必须连复数形式一起记，不能假设统一加一个词尾。
