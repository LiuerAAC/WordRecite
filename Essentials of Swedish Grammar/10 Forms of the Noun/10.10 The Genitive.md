# 10.10 The Genitive

## Source Correspondence

This section introduces the Swedish genitive. The genitive is normally formed by adding `-s`.

## Pattern

| Base | Genitive | English |
|---|---|---|
| Lisa | Lisas | Lisa's |
| pojken | pojkens | the boy's |
| Sverige | Sveriges | Sweden's |

## Key Point

The genitive `-s` is attached to the end of the whole noun phrase.

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

瑞典语所有格通常加 `-s`。它接在名词或名词短语末尾，功能类似英语所有格。
