# 10.4 Countable And Uncountable Nouns

## Source Correspondence

This section distinguishes nouns that can normally be counted from nouns that denote substances or abstract quantities.

## Contrast

| Type | Meaning | Example Type |
|---|---|---|
| Countable | individual items | chair, book, apple |
| Uncountable | substance or mass | water, coffee, iron |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

可数名词可形成普通复数；不可数名词通常表示材料、液体或抽象量。具体用法要和冠词、复数规则一起学。
