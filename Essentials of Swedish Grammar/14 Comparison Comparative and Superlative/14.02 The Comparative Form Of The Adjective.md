# 14.2 The Comparative Form Of The Adjective

## Source Correspondence

This section presents the regular comparative form of adjectives.

## Regular Pattern

```text
adjective + -are
```

| Positive | Comparative | English |
|---|---|---|
| billig | billigare | cheaper |
| snabb | snabbare | faster |
| intressant | intressantare | more interesting |

## With `än`

The comparative is often followed by `än`.

| Swedish | English |
|---|---|
| Den här boken är billigare än den där. | This book is cheaper than that one. |
| Maria är yngre än Peter. | Maria is younger than Peter. |

## Agreement

The comparative form does not change for gender or number.

| Subject | Predicate |
|---|---|
| Bilen är snabbare. |
| Huset är snabbare. |
| Bilarna är snabbare. |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

规则比较级常用 `-are`，并且比较级形式通常不随名词性数变化。`än` 表示“比”。
