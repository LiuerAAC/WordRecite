# 14.6 Making The Comparative And The Superlative With Mer And Mest

## Source Correspondence

This section explains comparison formed with `mer` and `mest`.

## Pattern

| Degree | Swedish Pattern | English |
|---|---|---|
| positive | komplicerad | complicated |
| comparative | mer komplicerad | more complicated |
| superlative | mest komplicerad | most complicated |

## When To Use `mer/mest`

`mer` and `mest` are common with longer adjectives and many participial adjectives.

| Positive | Comparative | Superlative |
|---|---|---|
| intresserad | mer intresserad | mest intresserad |
| förvånad | mer förvånad | mest förvånad |
| praktisk | mer praktisk | mest praktisk |

## Agreement

The adjective still follows normal adjective agreement where required.

| Swedish | English |
|---|---|
| ett mer intressant problem | a more interesting problem |
| de mest intressanta problemen | the most interesting problems |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

长形容词和许多分词式形容词常用 `mer/mest` 构成比较：`mer intressant`、`mest komplicerad`。形容词本身在需要时仍按普通形容词规则变化。

### 检查点

- `mer` 对应 comparative 还是 superlative？
- `mest` 后的形容词是否完全不变化？
- 哪些形容词更适合用 `mer/mest`？
