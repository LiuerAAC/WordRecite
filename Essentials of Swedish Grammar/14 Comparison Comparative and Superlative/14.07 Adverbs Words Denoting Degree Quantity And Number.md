# 14.7 Adverbs. Words Denoting Degree, Quantity And Number

## Source Correspondence

This section extends comparison to adverbs and to words that denote degree, quantity and number.

## Degree And Quantity

| Positive | Comparative | Superlative | Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| mycket | mer | mest | much/more/most |
| lite | mindre | minst | little/less/least |
| många | fler | flest | many/more/most, countable |

## Examples

| Swedish | English | Note |
|---|---|---|
| Jag arbetar mycket. | I work a lot. | degree/amount |
| Hon arbetar mer än jag. | She works more than I do. | comparative |
| Han har flest böcker. | He has the most books. | countable plural |
| Vi behöver mindre tid. | We need less time. | quantity |

## Countable Vs Quantity

| Use | Comparative | Superlative |
|---|---|---|
| number of countable things | fler | flest |
| amount/degree | mer | mest |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

数量和程度词也有比较形式。`många` 的比较级/最高级是 `fler/flest`，用于可数名词；`mycket` 对应 `mer/mest`，用于数量或程度。
