# 17 Cleft And Existential Sentences

## Source Correspondence

This chapter introduces two important sentence patterns: cleft sentences and existential sentences.

## Core Patterns

| Type | Pattern | Function |
|---|---|---|
| cleft | Det är X som... | emphasizes X |
| existential | Det finns X... | says that X exists/is available |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

第 17 章介绍两个重要句型。Cleft sentence 用来突出某个成分；existential sentence 用 `det finns` 表示“有/存在”。
