# 16.4 Indirect Questions

## Source Correspondence

This section introduces indirect questions: questions embedded inside another clause.

## Direct Vs Indirect

| Direct Question | Indirect Question |
|---|---|
| Kommer han? | Jag undrar om han kommer. |
| Var bor hon? | Jag vet inte var hon bor. |
| Vad heter du? | Hon frågar vad du heter. |

## Word Order

An indirect question is a subordinate clause. It follows subordinate clause word order, not direct question word order.

| Type | Verb Position |
|---|---|
| direct question | question pattern |
| indirect question | subordinate clause pattern |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

Indirect question 是嵌入在另一个句子里的问题内容。它不是直接问句，所以词序按 subordinate clause 来处理。
