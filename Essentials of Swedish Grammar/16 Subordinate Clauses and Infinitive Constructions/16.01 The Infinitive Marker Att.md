# 16.1 The Infinitive Marker Att

## Source Correspondence

This section explains `att` as an infinitive marker.

## Infinitive Marker

```text
att + infinitive
```

| Swedish | English |
|---|---|
| att läsa | to read |
| att skriva | to write |
| att förstå | to understand |

## `att` Marker Vs `att` Conjunction

| Type | Example | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| infinitive marker | Jag vill lära mig att tala svenska. | to speak |
| conjunction | Jag tror att han kommer. | that he is coming |

## After Auxiliary Verbs

Many auxiliary verbs are followed directly by the infinitive without `att`.

| Swedish | English |
|---|---|
| Jag kan simma. | I can swim. |
| Hon måste gå. | She must go. |
| Vi vill stanna. | We want to stay. |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

`att` 可作不定式标记，相当于英语 to；也可作连词，相当于 that。许多助动词后直接接 infinitive，不加 `att`。

### 检查点

- `att läsa` 中的 `att` 是什么？
- `Jag tror att...` 中的 `att` 是什么？
- `kan/måste/vill` 后是否通常加 `att`？
