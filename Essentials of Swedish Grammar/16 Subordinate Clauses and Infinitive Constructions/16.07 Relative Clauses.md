# 16.7 Relative Clauses

## Source Correspondence

This section revisits relative clauses and their role in describing nouns.

## Basic Pattern

```text
noun phrase + relative clause
```

| Swedish | English |
|---|---|
| mannen som bor här | the man who lives here |
| boken som jag läser | the book that I am reading |
| huset där hon bor | the house where she lives |

## `som`

`som` is a common relative marker. It can refer to people or things.

| Role In Relative Clause | Example |
|---|---|
| subject | kvinnan som arbetar här |
| object | filmen som vi såg |

## Word Order

The relative clause is subordinate, so it follows subordinate clause word order.

| Swedish | English |
|---|---|
| mannen som inte kommer | the man who is not coming |
| boken som jag inte har läst | the book that I have not read |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

Relative clause 修饰前面的名词。`som` 很常见，可指人或物。由于 relative clause 是 subordinate clause，`inte` 等句子副词按从句位置排列。
