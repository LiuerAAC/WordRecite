# 16.8 Isolated Prepositions

## Source Correspondence

This section discusses prepositions that remain at the end of a relative or question construction.

## Pattern

In Swedish, a preposition can be stranded at the end of the clause.

| Swedish | English |
|---|---|
| Vem talar du med? | Who are you talking with? |
| mannen som jag talar med | the man I am talking with |
| stolen som hon sitter på | the chair she is sitting on |

## Why It Happens

The noun or pronoun governed by the preposition is moved or represented earlier in the structure, but the preposition stays in its ordinary position.

| Base Idea | Question / Relative |
|---|---|
| Jag talar med honom. | Vem talar du med? |
| Hon sitter på stolen. | stolen som hon sitter på |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

瑞典语常允许介词留在句末，尤其在疑问句和关系从句中：`Vem talar du med?`、`stolen som hon sitter på`。

### 检查点

- `med` 在 `Vem talar du med?` 中为什么留在句末？
- `stolen som hon sitter på` 中 `på` 对应哪个名词？
- 这种结构在英语中是否也常见？
