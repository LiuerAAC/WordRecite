# 16.5 Indirect Yes/No Questions

## Source Correspondence

This section explains indirect yes/no questions, introduced by `om`.

## Pattern

```text
main clause + om + subordinate clause
```

| Direct Question | Indirect Question | English |
|---|---|---|
| Kommer han? | Jag undrar om han kommer. | I wonder whether he is coming. |
| Är hon sjuk? | Vet du om hon är sjuk? | Do you know whether she is ill? |
| Har de tid? | Vi frågar om de har tid. | We ask whether they have time. |

## Negation

In subordinate clauses, sentence adverbials such as `inte` usually come before the finite verb.

| Swedish | English |
|---|---|
| Jag vet inte om han inte kommer. | I do not know whether he is not coming. |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

间接 yes/no question 用 `om` 引导，相当于 whether/if。它后面是 subordinate clause，不是倒装问句。
