# 13.5 All, Hel, Annan, Sådan And Other Determiners

## Source Correspondence

This section surveys several common determiners used to express quantity, totality, difference and type.

## Common Forms

| Meaning | `en` Word | `ett` Word | Plural |
|---|---|---|---|
| all / whole | all | allt | alla |
| whole / entire | hel | helt | hela |
| other / another | annan | annat | andra |
| such | sådan | sådant | sådana |

## Examples

| Swedish | English | Note |
|---|---|---|
| all mat | all food | mass/uncountable use |
| allt vatten | all water | `ett` form |
| alla barn | all children | plural |
| en hel dag | a whole day | `hel` modifies a singular noun |
| ett helt år | a whole year | `ett` form |
| andra böcker | other books | plural form |
| en sådan bil | such a car | type/kind |

## Pattern Notes

| Determiner | Typical Function |
|---|---|
| `all/allt/alla` | total quantity |
| `hel/helt/hela` | whole unit |
| `annan/annat/andra` | another or different |
| `sådan/sådant/sådana` | such a kind |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

这一节的限定词都要注意性数变化。`all/allt/alla` 强调“全部”；`hel/helt/hela` 强调整体单位；`annan/annat/andra` 表示“另一个/其他”；`sådan/sådant/sådana` 表示“这样的”。

### 检查点

- `all` 和 `hel` 的意义差别是什么？
- `andra` 既可以对应什么单词，也可以对应什么数？
- `sådant` 用在什么名词前？
