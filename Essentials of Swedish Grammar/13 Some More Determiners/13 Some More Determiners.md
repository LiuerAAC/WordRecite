# 13 Some More Determiners

## Source Correspondence

This chapter presents determiners that specify, identify, question, quantify or negate nouns.

## Determiner Position

```text
determiner + adjective + noun
```

Many determiners have separate forms for `en` words, `ett` words and plural nouns.
