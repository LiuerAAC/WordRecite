# 8.13 G K And Sk Before Front Vowels

## Source Correspondence

This section explains that `g`, `k`, and `sk` have special pronunciations before front vowels.

## Front And Back Vowels

| Front Vowels | Back Vowels |
|---|---|
| i, e, ö, ä, y | o, å, a, u |

## Before Front Vowels

| Letter(s) | Sound | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| g | j | ge, göra |
| k | tj | kyla, köra |
| sk | sj | skina, skön |

Before back vowels, these letters are pronounced in the usual way.

## After R And L

| Pattern | Sound | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| rg | rj | berg, torg |
| lg | lj | helg |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

`g/k/sk` 在前元音前读音改变：`g -> j`, `k -> tj`, `sk -> sj`。前元音包括 `i, e, ö, ä, y`。
