# 8.17 Capital And Small Letters

## Source Correspondence

This section explains Swedish capitalization. Swedish uses capital letters much like English, but with important exceptions.

## Nationality Words

Nationality words begin with a small letter in Swedish.

| Swedish | English |
|---|---|
| engelska | English |
| tyska | German |
| franska | French |
| finsk | Finnish |
| svensk | Swedish |
| greker | Greeks |

## Days, Months, Seasons

Names of days, months, and seasons begin with small letters.

| Swedish | English |
|---|---|
| fredag | Friday |
| vintern | winter |
| december | December |
| oktober | October |

## Titles Of Works

In names of films, plays, and books, only the first word is capitalized.

| Swedish Title Style | English |
|---|---|
| Gudarna måste vara tokiga | The Gods Must Be Mad |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

瑞典语大小写比英语少用大写。语言、国籍、星期、月份、季节通常小写；书名电影名等通常只首词大写。

### 检查点

- 是否知道 `engelska`, `svensk` 小写？
- 是否知道 `fredag`, `december` 小写？
- 是否能按瑞典语规则写作品标题？
