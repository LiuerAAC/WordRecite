# 8.6 Swedish Short Vowels

## Source Correspondence

This section explains that short vowels are not merely shorter versions of long vowels. Some short vowels have special qualities, especially short `u`, and short `e` often has the same pronunciation as short `ä`.

## Important Contrasts

| Contrast | Examples |
|---|---|
| long/short u | bus/buss, hus/hund, rusa/rusta, sluta/slutta |
| short e = short ä | sett/sätt |

## Other Long/Short Pairs

| Long | Short |
|---|---|
| vin | vinn |
| syl | sylt |
| föl | föll |
| rot | rott |
| hål | håll |
| hat | hatt |

## Unstressed Vowels

Short vowels keep distinct pronunciations even when unstressed; they are not reduced to a neutral `e`.

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

短元音不只是长元音缩短。短 `u` 特别重要；短 `e` 和短 `ä` 在多数地区相同。非重读元音也不弱化成统一的 e。

### 检查点

- 是否能听辨 `bus/buss`？
- 是否知道 short `e` 常等于 short `ä`？
- 是否知道瑞典语非重读元音不简单弱化？
