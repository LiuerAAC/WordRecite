# 8.9.1 Swedish Stops

## Source Correspondence

Stops are consonants where the airflow is completely blocked for an instant. Swedish stops are similar to English stops.

## Swedish Stops

| Voiceless | Voiced |
|---|---|
| p | b |
| t | d |
| k | g |

## Notes

`t` and `d` in Swedish are pronounced with the tongue against the back of the upper front teeth, slightly different from English.

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

Stops 是瞬间完全阻断气流的辅音。瑞典语有 `p/t/k` 和对应浊音 `b/d/g`。
