# 8.11 Consonant Combinations

## Source Correspondence

This section introduces Swedish consonant clusters. Swedish, like English, can have two or three consonants before a vowel. If there are three, the first is always `s`, as in `strand`.

## Important Patterns

| Pattern | Example | Note |
|---|---|---|
| kn | knä, knacka | `k` is pronounced before `n` word-initially. |
| gn | gnaga | `g` is pronounced before `n` word-initially. |
| gn after vowel | vagn, lugn | pronounced as `ng + n`. |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

瑞典语可有多个辅音连在元音前。词首 `kn`, `gn` 中的 `k/g` 要发音；元音后的 `gn` 读作 `ng+n`。

- 是否知道 `knä` 中 k 发音？
- 是否知道 `vagn/lugn` 的 `gn` 读法？
- 是否知道三辅音词首通常以 s 开头？
