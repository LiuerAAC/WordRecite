# 8.9 Swedish Consonants

## Source Correspondence

This section introduces the consonant system in general. Consonants differ by where the obstruction occurs, how the air is obstructed, and whether the sound is voiced or voiceless.

## Three Dimensions

| Dimension | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Place | Where the airflow is obstructed. |
| Manner | How the airflow is obstructed. |
| Voicing | Whether the vocal cords vibrate. |

The book then focuses on two important groups: stops and fricatives.
