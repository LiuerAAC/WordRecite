# 8.10 Other Consonants

## Source Correspondence

This section covers other Swedish consonants that are mostly close to English equivalents, with several important details.

## Consonants

| Sound | Example | Note |
|---|---|---|
| m | mat | close to English |
| n | natt | close to English |
| ng | säng, sång | pronounced like English `singer`, not `finger` |
| h | ha, hamn | close to English |
| j | ja, jo-jo | like English `y` in `yes` |
| l | lära | always a clear `l`, like English `love` |
| r | röd | usually slightly rolled; not dropped word-finally |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

`j` 类似英语 yes 的 y；`ng` 像 singer，不像 finger；瑞典语 `l` 总是清晰 l；词尾 `r` 不脱落。
