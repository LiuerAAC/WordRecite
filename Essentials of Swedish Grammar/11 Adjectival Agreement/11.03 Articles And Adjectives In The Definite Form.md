# 11.3 Articles And Adjectives In The Definite Form

## Source Correspondence

This section explains definite noun phrases with adjectives. Swedish uses a definite article before the adjective and a definite noun form.

## Pattern

| Type | Pattern | Example |
|---|---|---|
| en word | den + adjective-a + definite noun | den stora bilen |
| ett word | det + adjective-a + definite noun | det stora huset |

## Key Point

The adjective usually takes `-a` in definite noun phrases.

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

定名词短语常出现“双重定性”：前面有 `den/det/de`，名词本身也是定形式，形容词通常加 `-a`。

### 检查点

- 是否能写 `den stora bilen`？
- 是否知道 adjective definite form 通常是 `-a`？
- 是否注意名词也是 definite？
