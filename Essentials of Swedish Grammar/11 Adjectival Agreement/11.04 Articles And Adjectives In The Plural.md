# 11.4 Articles And Adjectives In The Plural

## Source Correspondence

This section covers plural noun phrases with adjectives. Plural adjectives generally take `-a`.

## Plural Patterns

| Form | Pattern | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Indefinite plural | adjective-a + plural noun | stora bilar |
| Definite plural | de + adjective-a + definite plural noun | de stora bilarna |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

复数形容词通常加 `-a`。定复数结构为 `de + adjective-a + definite plural noun`。

### 检查点

- 是否能写 `stora bilar`？
- 是否能写 `de stora bilarna`？
- 是否知道 plural adjective 通常用 `-a`？
