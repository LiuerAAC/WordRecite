# 11.10 The Inflection Of Certain Participles And Adjectives

## Source Correspondence

This section covers special inflection patterns for certain adjectives and participles. These forms do not always follow the most transparent base/`-t`/`-a` pattern.

## Learning Strategy

| Problem Type | Strategy |
|---|---|
| irregular adjective forms | learn as a set |
| participle spelling changes | record all agreement forms |
| uncertain `-t` form | check dictionary or examples |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

某些形容词和分词变化不规则或有拼写调整。处理方法是按完整形态组记录，而不是临时套规则。

### 检查点

- 是否为特殊形容词建表？
- 是否记录 participle 的 agreement forms？
- 是否知道遇到不确定形式要查证？
