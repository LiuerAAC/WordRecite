# 11 Adjectival Agreement

## Source Correspondence

This chapter explains how Swedish adjectives agree with nouns in gender, definiteness, and number. It covers attributive and predicative adjectives, indefinite and definite noun phrases, plural forms, participle agreement, and special adjective forms.

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

第 11 章核心是 adjective agreement。形容词形式取决于名词短语的性、数、定性，以及形容词是放在名词前还是作表语。

### 学习建议

- 用 `en/ett/plural/definite` 四格表背形容词。
- `liten` 单独背。
- 分词作形容词时也要检查一致关系。
