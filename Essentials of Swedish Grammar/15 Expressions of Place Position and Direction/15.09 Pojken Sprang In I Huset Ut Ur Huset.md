# 15.9 Pojken Sprang In I Huset/Ut Ur Huset

## Source Correspondence

This section focuses on the contrast between movement into a place and movement out of a place.

## Core Contrast

| Swedish | English | Direction |
|---|---|---|
| Pojken sprang in i huset. | The boy ran into the house. | outside -> inside |
| Pojken sprang ut ur huset. | The boy ran out of the house. | inside -> outside |

## Structure

| Expression | Parts | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| in i | particle + preposition | into |
| ut ur | particle + preposition | out of |

## More Examples

| Swedish | English |
|---|---|
| Katten gick in i köket. | The cat went into the kitchen. |
| Barnen sprang ut ur skolan. | The children ran out of the school. |
| Hon tittade in i rummet. | She looked into the room. |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

`in i` 表示“进入到……里面”，`ut ur` 表示“从……里面出来”。这组表达清楚地区分起点和终点。

- `in i huset` 的起点和终点是什么？
- `ut ur huset` 的起点和终点是什么？
- 为什么单独 `i huset` 不等于 “into the house”？
