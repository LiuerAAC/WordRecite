# 15.2 Verbs Denoting Position

## Source Correspondence

This section introduces common verbs used to describe position.

## Position Verbs

| Verb | Basic Meaning | Typical Use |
|---|---|---|
| stå | stand | upright objects, things placed standing |
| sitta | sit | seated people, attached/fixed things |
| ligga | lie | flat or horizontal things |
| bo | live, reside | residence |
| vara | be | general location |

## Examples

| Swedish | English | Note |
|---|---|---|
| Boken ligger på bordet. | The book is lying on the table. | flat position |
| Glaset står på bordet. | The glass is on the table. | upright position |
| Nyckeln sitter i dörren. | The key is in the door. | attached/fixed position |
| De bor i Stockholm. | They live in Stockholm. | residence |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

瑞典语常用具体姿态动词表达位置：直立用 `stå`，坐着或固定着用 `sitta`，平放或躺着用 `ligga`。`vara` 是更泛化的“在”。
