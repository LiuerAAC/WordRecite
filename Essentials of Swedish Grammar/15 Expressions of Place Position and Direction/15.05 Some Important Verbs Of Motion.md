# 15.5 Some Important Verbs Of Motion

## Source Correspondence

This section introduces common verbs of motion.

## Motion Verbs

| Verb | Basic Meaning | Typical Use |
|---|---|---|
| gå | go, walk | movement on foot; also general going in some contexts |
| åka | go by vehicle | travel by car, bus, train, etc. |
| komma | come | motion toward speaker/reference point |
| resa | travel | longer or organized travel |
| springa | run | running |
| flyga | fly | by plane or flying |

## Examples

| Swedish | English |
|---|---|
| Jag går hem. | I am walking home. |
| Vi åker till Uppsala. | We are going to Uppsala. |
| Kommer du hit i kväll? | Are you coming here tonight? |
| Hon reser ofta till Finland. | She often travels to Finland. |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

`gå` 常表示步行，`åka` 常表示乘交通工具去，`komma` 表向说话者或参照点移动，`resa` 偏“旅行”。这些动词常和方向表达搭配。

- 乘火车去通常用 `gå` 还是 `åka`？
