# 15.7 Preposition Of Position Instead Of Preposition Of Direction

## Source Correspondence

This section notes that Swedish may sometimes use a position preposition where English learners expect an explicitly directional expression.

## General Idea

The verb or particle can already express motion, so the preposition can identify the final location.

| Swedish Pattern | Literal Structure | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| gå in i rummet | go in into the room | enter the room |
| lägga boken på bordet | lay the book on the table | put the book onto the table |
| sätta nyckeln i låset | set the key in the lock | put the key into the lock |

## Learning Strategy

Treat Swedish place expressions as complete patterns:

```text
motion/placement verb + particle/preposition + noun phrase
```

Do not translate every English to/into/onto mechanically.

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

瑞典语不总是逐字对应英语的 to/into/onto。动作动词或小品词已经表示方向时，介词可能表达终点位置。学习时要记完整搭配。

- 为什么不能总把英语 into 机械翻成一个固定词？
