# 15.8 Particles

## Source Correspondence

This section introduces particles used with verbs of motion and position.

## Common Particles

| Particle | Direction / Meaning | Example |
|---|---|---|
| in | inward | gå in |
| ut | outward | gå ut |
| upp | upward | gå upp |
| ner | downward | gå ner |
| bort | away | gå bort |
| hem | homeward | gå hem |
| fram | forward / up to | gå fram |
| tillbaka | back | gå tillbaka |

## Particle + Preposition

Particles often combine with prepositions.

| Swedish | English |
|---|---|
| gå in i huset | go into the house |
| gå ut ur rummet | go out of the room |
| gå upp på vinden | go up into the attic |
| gå ner från berget | go down from the mountain |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

Particles 表示方向或动作路径，如 `in`、`ut`、`upp`、`ner`。它们常和介词组合成更完整的方向表达：`in i`、`ut ur`。

### 检查点

- `gå ut` 和 `gå ut ur rummet` 的信息量差别是什么？
- `hem` 是名词还是方向副词？
- `tillbaka` 表示什么方向？
