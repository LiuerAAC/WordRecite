# 5.4 Word Order In Clauses With Pronouns

## Source Correspondence

This section explains where pronoun objects are placed in Swedish clauses. Without negation, a pronoun object occupies the same object position as a noun object. With `inte`, pronoun objects usually come before `inte`.

## Pronoun Objects In Object Position

| Subject | Verb | Object | English |
|---|---|---|---|
| Eva | fick | ett brev. | Eva got a letter. |
| Hon | läste | det genast. | She read it at once. |
| Per | känner | Maria. | Per knows Maria. |
| Han | träffade | henne i London. | He met her in London. |
| Erik | rakar | sig varje morgon. | Erik shaves every morning. |
| Jag | talade med | dem på telefon. | I spoke to them on the phone. |

## With Inte

| Noun Object | Pronoun Object |
|---|---|
| Hon läste inte brevet. | Hon läste det inte. |
| Jag såg inte Per. | Jag såg honom inte. |
| Barberaren rakade inte Per. | Per rakade sig inte. |

## Contrastive Use

A pronoun object can come after `inte` when it is contrastive or emphatic.

| Swedish | English |
|---|---|
| Nej, jag känner inte honom. | No, I do not know him. |
| Men jag känner hans bror. | But I know his brother. |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

代词作宾语时，普通肯定句中和名词宾语占同一位置。但在含 `inte` 的句子中，代词宾语通常放在 `inte` 前：`Hon läste det inte`。若代词放在 `inte` 后，通常带对比或强调。

### 检查点

- 是否能比较 `Hon läste inte brevet` 与 `Hon läste det inte`？
- 是否知道代词宾语通常在 `inte` 前？
- 是否能解释 `inte honom` 的强调效果？
