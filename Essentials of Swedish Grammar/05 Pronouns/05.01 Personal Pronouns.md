# 5.1 Personal Pronouns

## Source Correspondence

This section introduces the objective forms of Swedish personal pronouns. The subject forms were introduced in 2.6; here the same pronouns are shown in their object forms.

## Examples

| Swedish | English |
|---|---|
| Jag älskar dig. | I love you. |
| Älskar du mig? | Do you love me? |
| Jag ser honom. | I can see him. |
| Vi ser henne. | We can see her. |
| De ser inte oss. | They cannot see us. |
| Men vi ser dem. | But we can see them. |

## Subjective And Objective Forms

| Subjective Form | English | Objective Form | English |
|---|---|---|---|
| jag | I | mig / mej | me |
| du | you | dig / dej | you |
| han | he | honom | him |
| hon | she | henne | her |
| den | it, en words | den | it |
| det | it, ett words | det | it |
| vi | we | oss | us |
| ni | you | er | you |
| de / dom | they | dem / dom | them |

## Spoken Forms

| Standard | Colloquial | English |
|---|---|---|
| Jag älskar dig. | Jag älskar dej. | I love you. |
| Älskar du mig? | Älskar du mej? | Do you love me? |
| De kommer imorgon. | Dom kommer imorgon. | They are coming tomorrow. |
| Jag ser dem. | Jag ser dom. | I can see them. |

## After Prepositions

The same objective form is used after prepositions and after verbs followed by prepositions.

| Swedish | English |
|---|---|
| Han talar alltid om henne. | He is always talking about her. |
| Han väntade på henne flera timmar. | He waited for her for several hours. |
| Han talade länge med henne. | He spoke to her for a long time. |
| Kalle är mycket förtjust i henne. | Kalle is very keen on her. |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

人称代词作宾语时有宾格形式，如 `jag -> mig`, `du -> dig`, `han -> honom`, `hon -> henne`。口语中 `mig/dig` 常为 `mej/dej`，`de/dem` 常为 `dom`。

### 检查点

- 是否能背出主格和宾格对应表？
- 是否知道介词后也用宾格？
- 是否知道 `dom` 可对应 `de` 和 `dem`？
