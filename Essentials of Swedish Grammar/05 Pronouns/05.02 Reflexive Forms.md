# 5.2 Reflexive Forms

## Source Correspondence

This section introduces Swedish reflexive forms. Unlike English, Swedish generally uses the ordinary objective forms as reflexives, except in the third person, where the special form `sig` is used.

## Non-Third-Person Reflexives

| Swedish | English |
|---|---|
| Jag tvättar mig. | I wash myself. |
| Du tvättar dig. | You wash yourself. |
| Vi tvättar oss. | We wash ourselves. |
| Ni tvättar er. | You wash yourselves. |

## Third-Person Reflexive Sig

| Swedish | English |
|---|---|
| Han tvättar sig. | He is washing himself. |
| Hon klär på sig. | She is dressing herself. |
| De rakar sig. | They are shaving themselves. |
| Hon kammar sig. | She is combing her hair. |
| Vi måste skynda oss. | We have to hurry. |

Many Swedish verbs with reflexive pronouns correspond to English verbs where the reflexive is optional or absent.

## Reflexive Forms Table

| Subjective | Objective | Reflexive |
|---|---|---|
| jag | mig | mig |
| du | dig | dig |
| han | honom | sig |
| hon | henne | sig |
| den | den | sig |
| det | det | sig |
| vi | oss | oss |
| ni | er | er |
| de | dem | sig |

## Colloquial Form

| Standard | Colloquial |
|---|---|
| Per tvättar sig. | Per tvättar sej. |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

瑞典语反身代词在第一、第二人称和复数 `vi/ni` 中多用普通宾格；第三人称统一用 `sig`，可对应 himself, herself, itself, themselves。

### 检查点

- 是否知道第三人称反身形式是 `sig`？
- 是否能区分 `Jag kammar henne` 和 `Hon kammar sig`？
- 是否知道 `sig` 的口语写法可为 `sej`？
