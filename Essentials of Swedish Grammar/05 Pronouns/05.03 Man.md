# 5.3 Man

## Source Correspondence

This section introduces the Swedish pronoun `man`, meaning one, you, they, people, or we depending on context. It is used when no particular person is intended, or when the statement concerns people in general.

## Uses Of Man

| Swedish | English |
|---|---|
| Man blir trött om man sover för mycket. | One/you get tired if one/you sleep too much. |
| I Sverige dricker man mycket kaffe. | They drink a lot of coffee in Sweden. |
| På vintern åker man ofta skidor. | In the winter people often go skiing. |
| Man ser sjön från balkongen. | One/you/we can see the lake from the balcony. |

## Forms

| Subjective Form | Objective Form | Reflexive Form |
|---|---|---|
| man | en | sig |

## Examples

| Swedish | English |
|---|---|
| Ingen gillar en, om man skryter. | No one likes you if you boast. |
| Man frågar sig, varför det hände. | One asks oneself why it happened. |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

`man` 表示泛指的人，可译作 one, you, they, people, we。宾格是 `en`，反身形式是 `sig`。

### 检查点

- 是否能解释 `man` 为什么不是特指某个人？
- 是否记住 `man / en / sig`？
- 是否能用 `I Sverige ... man ...` 造句？
