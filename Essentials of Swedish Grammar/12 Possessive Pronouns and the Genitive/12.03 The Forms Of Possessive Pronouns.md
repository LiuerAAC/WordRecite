# 12.3 The Forms Of Possessive Pronouns

## Source Correspondence

This section gives the forms of Swedish possessive pronouns and shows which forms agree with the possessed noun.

## Form Table

| Possessor | `en` Word | `ett` Word | Plural | English |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| jag | min | mitt | mina | my |
| du | din | ditt | dina | your |
| subject of same clause | sin | sitt | sina | his/her/its/their own |
| vi | vår | vårt | våra | our |
| ni | er | ert | era | your |

## Invariable Forms

| Possessor | Form | English |
|---|---|---|
| han | hans | his |
| hon | hennes | her |
| den / det | dess | its |
| de | deras | their |

## Examples

| `en` Word | `ett` Word | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| min bok | mitt bord | mina böcker |
| din vän | ditt barn | dina vänner |
| vår skola | vårt hus | våra skolor |
| er lärare | ert rum | era lärare |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

有变化的所有代词按被拥有物的性和数变化：`min bok`、`mitt hus`、`mina böcker`。不变形式如 `hans/hennes/deras` 不根据名词变化。

### 检查点

- `vår`, `vårt`, `våra` 分别用于什么名词？
- `deras` 是否有 `derat` 或 `dera`？
- `sin` 的形式变化根据哪个名词？
