# 12 Possessive Pronouns And The Genitive

## Source Correspondence

This chapter brings together two ways of expressing possession in Swedish: possessive pronouns and the genitive `-s`.

## Core Pattern

```text
possessive / genitive + adjective + noun
```

| Type | Example | Note |
|---|---|---|
| Possessive pronoun | min nya bil | no final definite article on the noun |
| Genitive noun | Annas nya bil | the possessor takes `-s` |
| Reflexive possessive | Anna kör sin bil. | Anna drives her own car |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

第 12 章处理所有关系：possessive pronouns 表示“我的、你的、他的”等，genitive 用 `-s` 表示所有格。特别要注意 `sin/sitt/sina` 是反身所有代词，指回同一句中的主语。
