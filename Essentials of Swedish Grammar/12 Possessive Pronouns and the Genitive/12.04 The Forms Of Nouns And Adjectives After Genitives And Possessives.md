# 12.4 The Forms Of Nouns And Adjectives After Genitives And Possessives

## Source Correspondence

This section describes the form of the noun phrase after a possessive pronoun or a genitive noun phrase.

## Noun Phrase Pattern

```text
possessive/genitive + adjective + noun
```

The noun normally does not take the definite suffix after a possessive or a genitive.

| Type | Swedish | Not This | English |
|---|---|---|---|
| possessive | min bil | min bilen | my car |
| possessive + adjective | min nya bil | min ny bil | my new car |
| genitive | Annas bil | Annas bilen | Anna's car |
| genitive + adjective | Annas nya bil | Annas ny bil | Anna's new car |

## Adjective Form

An adjective after a possessive or genitive commonly has the definite-style `-a` form.

| Base Adjective | Phrase | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| ny | min nya bok | my new book |
| gammal | Peters gamla cykel | Peter's old bicycle |
| stor | vår stora lägenhet | our large apartment |

## Genitive Reminder

The genitive `-s` is attached to the possessor.

| Possessor | Genitive Phrase |
|---|---|
| Anna | Annas bok |
| pojken | pojkens hund |
| min syster | min systers lärare |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

所有代词或 genitive 后，名词通常不用 definite suffix：说 `min bil`，不是 `min bilen`。如果中间有形容词，形容词常用 definite-style `-a`：`min nya bil`、`Annas gamla hus`。

### 检查点

- 为什么 `min bilen` 不对？
- `min ny bil` 和 `min nya bil` 哪个是标准形式？
- genitive `-s` 加在什么位置？
