# 9.16 Verbs With Two Objects

## Source Correspondence

This section introduces verbs that can take two objects: an indirect object, often the receiver, and a direct object, the thing given, shown, sent, etc.

## Object Pattern

```text
Subject + verb + indirect object + direct object
```

| Swedish Pattern | English |
|---|---|
| Jag gav henne boken. | I gave her the book. |
| Han skickade mig ett brev. | He sent me a letter. |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

有些动词可接两个宾语：间接宾语通常是接受者，直接宾语是被给予/发送/展示的东西。

### 检查点

- 是否能区分 direct object 和 indirect object？
- 是否能造句：`Jag gav henne...`？
- 是否能识别两个宾语的顺序？
