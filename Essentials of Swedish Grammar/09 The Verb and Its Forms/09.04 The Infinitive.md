# 9.4 The Infinitive

## Source Correspondence

The infinitive is the form normally found in dictionaries. Most Swedish infinitives end in `-a`, though there are exceptions, especially short verbs.

## Examples

| Infinitive | English |
|---|---|
| arbeta | work |
| läsa | read |
| köpa | buy |
| bo | live |
| gå | go |

## Use

The infinitive often follows auxiliary verbs.

| Swedish | English |
|---|---|
| Jag kan läsa. | I can read. |
| Hon vill köpa bilen. | She wants to buy the car. |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

不定式是词典形式，多数以 `-a` 结尾。它常出现在助动词之后，如 `kan läsa`, `vill köpa`。
