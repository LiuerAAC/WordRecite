# 9.15 The Past Participle

## Source Correspondence

This section explains past participle formation. The past participle is formed from the basic form by adding `-d`, `-t`, or `-dd`, depending on the verb.

## Examples

| Basic Form | Past Participle | English |
|---|---|---|
| intressera | intresserad | interested |
| öka | ökad | increased |
| glöm | glömd | forgotten |
| stäng | stängd | closed |

## Use

Past participles can be used as adjectives and therefore interact with adjective agreement.
