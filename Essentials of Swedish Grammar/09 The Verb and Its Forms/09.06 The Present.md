# 9.6 The Present

## Source Correspondence

This section systematizes the Swedish present tense. Present forms normally end in `-r`, either as `-ar` or `-er`, though some verbs are irregular or short.

## Common Present Endings

| Verb Type | Present Ending | Example |
|---|---|---|
| `-ar` verbs | `-ar` | arbetar |
| `-er` verbs | `-er` | läser, köper |
| short/irregular verbs | varied | går, får, är |

## English Contrast

Swedish present tense can correspond to both English simple present and continuous present.

| Swedish | English |
|---|---|
| Lisa arbetar nu. | Lisa is working now. |
| Lisa arbetar varje dag. | Lisa works every day. |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

瑞典语现在时通常以 `-r` 结尾，可对应英语 simple present 和 continuous present。
