# 9.13 The Participle Forms Of The Verb

## Source Correspondence

This section introduces the two participle forms: present participle and past participle. Participles can be used similarly to adjectives.

## Present And Past Participles

| Type | Swedish Example | Equivalent Clause |
|---|---|---|
| Present participle | en läsande pojke | en pojke som läser |
| Present participle | en skrattande kvinna | en kvinna som skrattar |
| Past participle | en stängd dörr | en dörr som någon har stängt |
| Past participle | en betald räkning | en räkning som man har betalat |

## Particles And Prefix O-

| Form | Meaning |
|---|---|
| avstängd | turned off |
| bortsprungen | run away, stray |
| oläst | unread |
| obetald | unpaid |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

分词包括 present participle 和 past participle，可像形容词一样修饰名词。带 particle 的动词构成分词时，particle 常与分词合写；前缀 `o-` 表示 not/un-。
