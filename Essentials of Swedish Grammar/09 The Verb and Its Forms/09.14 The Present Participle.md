# 9.14 The Present Participle

## Source Correspondence

This section explains how to form the present participle. It usually ends in `-ande`; after a long stressed vowel it uses `-ende`.

## Rules

| Condition | Ending | Example |
|---|---|---|
| ordinary basic form | `-ande` | arbetande, hängande, läsande |
| after long stressed vowel | `-ende` | gående, troende |

If the basic form ends in unstressed `-a`, that `a` disappears before `-ande`.

```text
öppna + ande -> öppnande
```

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

现在分词通常为基本形式 + `ande`；长重读元音后用 `ende`。非重读词尾 `a` 在加 `ande` 前脱落。
