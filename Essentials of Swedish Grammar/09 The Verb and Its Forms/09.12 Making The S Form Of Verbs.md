# 9.12 Making The S Form Of Verbs

## Source Correspondence

This section explains how to make `s` forms. The `s` is added to infinitive, present, past, and supine forms, with some spelling adjustments.

## Example Paradigm

| Form | Base | S Form | English |
|---|---|---|---|
| Infinitive | räkna | räknas | be counted |
| Present | räknar | räknas | is/are counted |
| Past | räknade | räknades | was/were counted |
| Supine | räknat | räknats | been counted |

## Non-Passive S Verbs

Some verbs always have `s` form without passive meaning.

| Verb | English |
|---|---|
| hoppas | hope |
| minnas | remember |
| trivas | enjoy life, get on |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

`s` 形式可用于被动，但也有一些本身就是 `s` 形式且不表示被动的动词，如 `hoppas`, `minnas`, `trivas`。

### 检查点

- 是否能写出 `räknas/räknades/räknats`？
- 是否知道 `hoppas` 不是被动？
- 是否能识别 present `-er` 动词加 `s` 的变化？
