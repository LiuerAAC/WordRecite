# 9.10 Irregular Verbs

## Source Correspondence

This section groups verbs whose forms cannot be predicted reliably from the regular patterns. Many of them are frequent and must be memorized.

## High-Frequency Irregulars

| Infinitive | Present | Past | Supine | English |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| vara | är | var | varit | be |
| ha | har | hade | haft | have |
| få | får | fick | fått | get, may |
| gå | går | gick | gått | go |
| göra | gör | gjorde | gjort | do, make |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

不规则动词高频且无法完全靠规则推出。最重要的是 `vara`, `ha`, `få`, `gå`, `göra` 等。

### 检查点

- 是否能背 `vara/är/var/varit`？
- 是否能背 `göra/gör/gjorde/gjort`？
- 是否建立了不规则动词表？
