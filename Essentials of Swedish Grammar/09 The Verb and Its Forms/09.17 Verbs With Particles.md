# 9.17 Verbs With Particles

## Source Correspondence

This section introduces verbs with particles. A particle is a small word that combines with a verb and changes or specifies its meaning. In Swedish, the particle is stressed.

## Examples

| Verb + Particle | Meaning |
|---|---|
| stänga av | turn off |
| springa bort | run away |
| tycka om | like |
| hålla på | be doing, continue |

## Key Point

Particles are important because the meaning of the whole expression cannot always be predicted from the verb alone.

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

Particle verbs 是动词加小品词构成的固定表达，particle 通常重读并改变整体意义。学习时应把整个短语作为词条记忆。
