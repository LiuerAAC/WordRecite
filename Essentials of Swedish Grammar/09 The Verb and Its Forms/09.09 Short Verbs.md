# 9.9 Short Verbs

## Source Correspondence

This section treats short verbs, many of which have short infinitives and compact forms. They are common and should be learned individually.

## Examples

| Infinitive | Present | Past | Supine | English |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| bo | bor | bodde | bott | live |
| tro | tror | trodde | trott | believe |
| nå | når | nådde | nått | reach |
| gå | går | gick | gått | go |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

短动词常见且形式紧凑，部分规则、部分不规则。建议把 infinitive, present, past, supine 四列一起背。

### 检查点

- 是否能背 `bo/bor/bodde/bott`？
- 是否知道 `gå/går/gick/gått` 是高频不规则？
- 是否能按四列表记录短动词？
