# 9.11 The Passive

## Source Correspondence

This section introduces the passive. Swedish often forms the passive by adding `-s` to the verb.

## Active And Passive

| Active | Passive | English |
|---|---|---|
| Någon öppnar dörren. | Dörren öppnas. | The door is opened. |
| Någon stängde fönstret. | Fönstret stängdes. | The window was closed. |

## Key Point

The `s` passive is common in Swedish. English often uses `be + past participle`, while Swedish can use an `s` form directly.

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

瑞典语被动常用 `s` 形式，如 `öppnas`, `stängdes`。这和英语 `be + past participle` 不完全对应。
