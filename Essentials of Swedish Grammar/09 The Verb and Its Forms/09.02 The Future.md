# 9.2 The Future

## Source Correspondence

This section explains that Swedish has several ways to refer to future time. The choice depends on meaning: plan, intention, prediction, or neutral futurity.

## Common Future Expressions

| Swedish Pattern | Typical Meaning | Example |
|---|---|---|
| present tense + future time expression | scheduled or contextually future | Jag reser imorgon. |
| ska + infinitive | plan, intention, future | Jag ska resa. |
| kommer att + infinitive | prediction, future development | Det kommer att regna. |
| tänker + infinitive | intention | Jag tänker stanna hemma. |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

瑞典语表达将来不只有一种形式。`ska` 常表示计划或将来，`kommer att` 偏预测，`tänker` 表示打算；有明确时间状语时现在时也可表示将来。

### 检查点

- 是否能区分 `ska` 和 `kommer att`？
- 是否能用现在时表达未来？
- 是否能造句：`Jag ska...`, `Jag tänker...`？
