# 9.1 The Perfect And The Pluperfect

## Source Correspondence

This section introduces the perfect (`perfekt`) and pluperfect (`pluskvamperfekt`). Swedish forms both with a form of `ha` plus the supine of the main verb.

## Forms

| Tense | Pattern | Example | English |
|---|---|---|---|
| Perfect | har + supine | Jag har varit sjuk. | I have been ill. |
| Pluperfect | hade + supine | Jag hade varit sjuk. | I had been ill. |
| Perfect | har + supine | Per har badat. | Per has had a bath. |
| Pluperfect | hade + supine | Per hade badat. | Per had had a bath. |

## Rule

```text
Perfect: har + supine
Pluperfect: hade + supine
```

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

完成时用 `har + supine`，过去完成时用 `hade + supine`。这里的主动词不是 past participle，而是瑞典语的 supine。
