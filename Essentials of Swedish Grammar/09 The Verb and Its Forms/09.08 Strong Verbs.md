# 9.8 Strong Verbs

## Source Correspondence

Strong verbs form the past tense by changing the vowel of the stem rather than by adding the usual weak past endings.

## Pattern

| Present | Past | English |
|---|---|---|
| skriver | skrev | writes / wrote |
| läser | läste | reads / read |
| kommer | kom | comes / came |
| sover | sov | sleeps / slept |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

强动词的过去时靠词干元音变化，不能只套 `-ade/-de/-te`。这些动词需要按组和例词记忆。
