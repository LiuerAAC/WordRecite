# 9.3 How To Make The Verb Forms

## Source Correspondence

This section introduces the systematic formation of Swedish verb forms. The imperative/basic form is central: other forms can often be made by adding endings to it.

## Main Verb Groups

| Type | Present | Past | Supine | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| `-ar` verbs | `-ar` | `-ade` | `-at` | very common regular group |
| `-er` verbs | `-er` | `-de` / `-te` | `-t` | depends on stem sound |
| strong verbs | often `-er` | vowel change | `-it` or other | must learn patterns |
| short verbs | short forms | special | special | common verbs |

## Study Notes / Summary

### 中文总结

本节建立动词变位总框架。瑞典语动词形式通常从基本形式加词尾得到，但不同动词组有不同规则，强变化和不规则动词需要单独记。
