# 9.6 The present

## Extracted Text

The present
There are two endings in the present (presens): r and er. If the basic form
ends in a, add an r. Otherwise, add er. (In this case the basic form always
ends in a consonant.)
BASIC FORM PRESENT
öppna + r — ► öppnar open(s)
fråga + r frågar ask(s)
ring + er ---- ► ringer ring(s)
känn + er känner know(s);
köp -1- er köper buy(s)
läs + er läser read(s)
The rule for forming the present is thus:
Present: basic form + r after a
basic form + er after a consonant
