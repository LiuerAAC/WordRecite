10.9 Plurals: summary
The most important rules for the noun in the plural are summarized in the
table below. It shows the formation of both the indefinite and the definite
forms in the plural.
SINGULAR PLURAL
INDEFINITE DEFINITE
‘En’ words
ending in a en gata gat^ or gator
a street na gatorna
ending in e en tjmme timm^ ar timmar
an hour na timmarna
with the stress en cigarett er cigaretter
on the last a cigarette na cigaretterna
vowel
‘Ett’ words
ending in ett möte n möten
a vowel a meeting a mötena
ending in a ett glas - glas
consonant a glass en glasen
