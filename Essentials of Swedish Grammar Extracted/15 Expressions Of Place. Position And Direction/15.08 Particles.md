15.8 Particles
In Swedish as in English there are a number of particles which are used to
denote position. Note that these particles, unlike prepositions, are stressed
(compare 9.17).
Here are the most important particles:
POSITION D IRECTIO N
Where? Where to? Where from ?
hemma hem hemifrån
at home home from home
borta bort bortifrån
away away from a long way away
inne in inifrån
inside, indoors in, inside, indoors from inside
ute Ut utifrån
outside, outdoors out, outside, outdoors from outside
uppe upp uppifrån
up (there) up from above
nere ner, ned nerifrån
down (there) down from below
framme fram framifrån
in front (here) forward, on from the front
Sten är ensam hemma. Föräldrar­ Sten is alone at home. His parents
na är borta hos en bekant. are away at a friend’s.
Eva måste gå hemifrån kl. 7 varje Eva has to leave home at
morgon. 7 o’clock every morning.
Hon kommer hem kl. 6. She gets home at 6 o’clock.
Maria står inne i köket och tittar Maria is standing in the kitchen
ut genom fönstret. looking out through the window.
Ute på gården leker några barn, Out in the yard some children are
som kastar upp stenar i luften. playing, throwing stones up in the air.
Gå in genom den högra dörren och Go in through the right-hand door
ut genom den vänstra! and out through the left-hand one.
Olle bor uppe på vinden. Olle lives up in the attic.
Vi förvarar allt gammalt skräp We keep all the old rubbish down
nere i källaren. in the cellar.
When you talk about places on a map, uppe and nere are often used in a
special way, just as in English. Uppe is used about places north of the place
you are in, and nere about places south of where you are:
De bor nere i Malmö. They live down in Malmö.
Sommaren tillbringar de uppe i They spend the summer up in the
fjällen. mountains.
Jag tänker åka ner till Göteborg I am going down to G othenburg
i övermorgon. the day after tomorrow.

tillbaka ‘back’
Jag går nu. Jag kommer tillbaka I’m going now. I’ll be back
om en timme. in an hour.
Du måste lämna tillbaka böckerna You must take the books back to
till biblioteket. the library.
Kan du ge tillbaka på en Can you change a hundred
hundralapp? crown note?
