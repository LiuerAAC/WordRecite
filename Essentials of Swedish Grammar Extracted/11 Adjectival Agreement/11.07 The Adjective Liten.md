# 11.7 The adjective liten

## Extracted Text

11.7 The adjective liten
The adjective liten ‘little’, ‘small’ changes its form in a different way from
other adjectives. In the plural the word is replaced by another word, små.
INDEFINITE DEFINITE
PREDICATIVE FORM FORM
SINGULAR Skjortan är liten. en liten skjorta den lilla skjortan
The shirt is small. a small shirt the small shirt
Skåpet är litet. ett litet skåp det lilla skåpet
The cupboard is small. a small cupboard the small cupboard
PLURAL Skjortorna är små. två små skjortor de små skåpen
The shirts are small. two small shirts the small cupboards
