# 11.8 Agreement of the participles

## Extracted Text

11.8 Agreement of the participles
As we saw in 9.13 the participle forms of the verb may function as adjectives.
The past participle (9.15) agrees with its noun in the same way as an
adjective by adding t and a:
INDEFINITE DEFINITE
PREDICATIVE FORM FORM
SINGULAR Dörren är stängd. en stängd dörr den stängda dörren
The door is closed. a closed door the closed door
Fönstret är stängt. ett stängt fönster det stängda fönstret
The window is closed. a closed window the closed window
PLURAL Dörrarna är stängda, två stängda dörrar de stängda dörrarna
The doors are closed. two closed doors the closed doors

The present participle, which ends in ande or ende, does not change its form:
Informationen var uppmuntrande. The information was encouraging.
Meddelandet var uppmuntrande, The message was encouraging,
ett uppmuntrande meddelande an encouraging message
det uppmuntrande meddelandet the encouraging message
